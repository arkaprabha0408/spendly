Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details — your name, email address, and hashed password.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • Display your expense history, summaries, and spending breakdowns.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.
  • Send transactional emails such as password resets, if applicable.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorised access, including password hashing, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access controls.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use the Service at your own risk, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers may process your data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies. We only work with providers that offer adequate data protection standards.

We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics trackers, or social media plugins that would share your data with those parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also withdraw consent to data processing at any time by deleting your account.

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the GDPR or UK GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
